Account Deletion Instructions for Ballhola



Thank you for using Ballhola. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from our system, please follow these steps:
  1. Open Ballhola: Launch the Ballhola application on your device.
  2. Navigate to Settings: Tap on the "Profile" option within the app. This is represented in the left bottom of your screen.
  3. Find Delete Account Option: Scroll through the Account Settings until you find the "Delete Account" option.
  4. Confirm Account Deletion: Tap on the "Delete Account" option. You will be asked to confirm your decision.
  5. Submit Deletion Request: Once you are ready to proceed, tap on the “Yes, delete” button to submit your deletion request.
